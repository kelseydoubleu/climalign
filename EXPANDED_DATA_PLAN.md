Base: 108°F

- Envelope: -11°F
- Roof: -8°F
Total reduction: -42°F
Final: 66°F... wait that's too cold.

Actually, these stack multiplicatively not additively:
- Cooling prevents 23°F of interior warming
- Envelope prevents 11°F of heat gain

Realistic calculation:

With comprehensive retrofits:
- Envelope reduces gain: 108°F → 98°F (-10°F)
- Cooling brings down: 98°F → 78°F (-20°F)
- Cool roof helps: Additional -3°F benefit
Final: ~75-80°F = ALIGNED ✓
